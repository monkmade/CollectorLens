CollectorLens — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08 · Version 0.1.0

CollectorLens does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. It has no backend server, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

What the extension does

CollectorLens runs entirely in your browser. On eBay comic listings it reads the listing title that is already on the page, and it requests eBay's own public "Sold / Completed listings" search within your current browser session in order to compute a price range for the same issue and grade. This request goes only to www.ebay.com.

Data collection

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No browsing history is recorded or transmitted.
  • No data is sent to the developer or any third party.
  • No cookies are set by the extension.
  • A small in-memory cache of computed price ranges exists only while the page is open and is discarded afterwards.

Permissions

The extension requests access to https://www.ebay.com/* only — solely to read the listing you are viewing and to query eBay's own public sold-listings. No other sites are accessed.

Third parties

CollectorLens does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. Price data is read from eBay's own public pages; eBay's own privacy terms apply to your use of eBay.
